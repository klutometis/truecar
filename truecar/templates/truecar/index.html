Vehicle search

Notes

The following implements a prefix-based lookahead which is order-sensitive (year, make, model, body). "2012 ben", for instance, will match "2012 Bentley Continental GT Coupe" and "2012 Bentley Mulsanne Sedan." On the other hand, "ben 2012" won't match anything.

At least one record in the database was defective (2014 Chevrolet Impala Sedan); I did my best to swap in some reasonable defaults.

Search

  1. Start typing by year;
  2. select a model from the drop-down to activate the details.
